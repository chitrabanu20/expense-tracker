** About Expense Tracker **

Expense Tracker is a web application designed to help you manage your finances more effectively. Whether you're tracking your monthly expenses, setting budget goals, or analyzing your spending habits, Expense Tracker has you covered.

Key features of Expense Tracker include:

  1. Interface for tracking expenses
  2. Customizable budget categories
  3. Detailed expense reports and analytics
  4. User-friendly login and authentication system
